## 1. Introduction

Welcome to CPA Exam Study Guide ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand how your personal information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you use our mobile application ("App").

This Privacy Policy applies to the CPA Exam Study Guide iOS application and describes our practices regarding the collection, use, disclosure, and protection of your information.

**By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.**

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you use our App, you may provide the following information:

- **Account Information**: Email address, display name, and profile picture (if you choose to sign in with Google or Apple)
- **Study Preferences**: Your CPA exam timeline, current study status, primary focus section, and daily study goals
- **Study Progress**: Answers to practice questions, quiz results, study session data, and performance metrics

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use our App, we automatically collect:

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: Features used, time spent studying, questions answered, and app interactions
- **Analytics Data**: Crash reports, performance metrics, and general usage patterns

### 2.3 Information from Third-Party Services

If you choose to sign in using third-party services:

- **Google Sign-In**: We receive your email address, name, and profile picture from your Google account
- **Apple Sign-In**: We receive your email address (or a private relay email) and name from your Apple ID

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- **Provide Core App Functionality**: Enable study modes, track your progress, and personalize your learning experience
- **Personalize Your Experience**: Recommend study content based on your performance and goals
- **Implement Spaced Repetition**: Schedule question reviews based on your retention patterns
- **Track Progress and Analytics**: Show you insights about your study habits and improvement areas
- **Process Subscriptions**: Manage your subscription status through Apple's App Store
- **Send Notifications**: Deliver study reminders and achievement notifications (with your permission)
- **Improve Our Services**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance app features and fix issues
- **Provide Customer Support**: Respond to your inquiries and resolve issues

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Where Your Data is Stored

Your data is stored using:

- **Firebase (Google Cloud Platform)**: Our primary backend service, hosted in the United States
- **Local Device Storage**: Certain preferences and cached data are stored locally on your device for offline access

### 4.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

- Encryption of data in transit using TLS/SSL
- Secure authentication through Firebase Authentication
- Regular security assessments and updates
- Access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel only

### 4.3 Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: We retain your data for as long as your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts**: Upon account deletion request, we delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law
- **Anonymous Usage Data**: Aggregated, anonymized analytics data may be retained indefinitely for service improvement

## 6. Auto-Renewable Subscriptions

### 6.1 Subscription Information

Our App offers the following auto-renewable subscription options:

- **Pro Monthly**: Billed monthly, auto-renews every month
- **Pro Annual**: Billed annually, auto-renews every year
- **Pro Lifetime**: One-time purchase, no renewal

### 6.2 Payment and Billing

- All payments are processed by Apple through the App Store
- We do not have access to your payment information (credit card, banking details)
- Subscription prices are displayed in the App before purchase
- Subscriptions automatically renew unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period

### 6.3 Managing Your Subscription

You can manage or cancel your subscription through:
- iOS Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions
- The App Store app on your device
