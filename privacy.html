2. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our App. Here's exactly what we collect:

2.1 Account Information

  • Anonymous User ID: Automatically generated unique identifier if you use the App without signing in
  • Email Address: Provided when you sign in with Google
  • Display Name: Your name from your Google account (if you use Google Sign-In)
  • Profile Picture: Your Google profile picture URL (if you use Google Sign-In)

2.2 Study Progress Data

  • Questions Answered: Which practice questions you've completed
  • Test Results: Your scores, correct/incorrect answers, and performance by topic
  • Flashcard Progress: Cards marked as "known" or "study again"

  • Spaced Repetition Data: Next review dates, ease factors, and repetition intervals
  • Study Preferences: Daily study goals, reminder settings, focus sections

2.3 Gamification Data

  • Points and Levels: Total points earned and current level
  • Achievements: Unlocked achievements and progress toward achievement goals
  • Streaks: Current study streak and longest streak
  • Leaderboard Data: Anonymous rankings (if you opt-in to leaderboards)

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Encryption at Rest: Firebase automatically encrypts data stored in Firestore databases
  • Access Controls: Firebase Security Rules ensure users can only access their own data
  • Secure Token Storage: Authentication tokens are securely stored on your device using iOS Keychain

Important: While we implement industry-standard security measures, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.
